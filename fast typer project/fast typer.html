How to play

One-by-one, a random word will pop-up on your screen and you have to type the word within a given amount of seconds else the game ends. When you enter the word before the clock gets to 0, the clock wll restart and a new word will display. Once the clock hits 0, your final score is displayed and it's game over.
Enjoy and Keep Typing :-)
